Out of Exile

It is the second album by the American hard rock band. Audioslave, released on May 23, 2005 in Europe and a day later in the United States. It is the group's only album to reach first place on the American charts, releasing four singles: "Be Yourself", "Your Time Has Come", "No Te Reminder of Me" and "Out of Exile". The album was warmly received by critics, considering that the band was seeking to define its own sound, separating itself from the bands from which its members came (Rage Against the Machine and Soundgarden). "You don't remember me" was nominated for the awards. 2006 Grammy in the category of "Best Hard Rock Performance."
